Dontogan can be considered as a pretty decent barangay in Baguio. Although it is a little far from the city, the trip will be worth it. The environment compared to the city is entirely different, you would think as though you are in the countryside. It wouldn't be considered entirely free of trash and pollution but its still better as compared to your stereotypical city.
Not to mention the community clean up, several projects can also be seen to have been implemented for the sake of its people. It can be seen that a public basketball court and a playground was built. These facilities provide the people, especially the young ones, a good livelihood.
The welfare of the community can be attributed to the responsible governance of its local administration.

Barangay Dontogan is located in Baguio City. To be more specific, it is the south-westernmost barangay of the city. It lies in the Green Valley subdivision.

Travel time from Baguio to Dontogan would be about 17 min.
