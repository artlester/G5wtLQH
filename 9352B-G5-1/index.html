Nestled at the panoramic and famous Mount Sto. Tomas, Baguio City's highest summit at 5000 feet above sea level-is a community of people who adapted to their environment and to the demands of an ever-changing world keeping alive their own values and traditions from mixed cultures and heritages.

This community of good natured people is called Dontogan, this place is a bit far from the city of Baguio but its nature as a clean and peaceful barangay stands. The state and welfare of the community can be attributed to the good and responsible governance of its local administration.

Dontogan Barangay is governed by the Barangay Council headed by the Punong Barangay, 7 Kagawads with their respective Committees, Barangay Secretary and Barangay Treasurer. They are the elected and appointed Barangay officials of Dontogan Barangay.Generally, the Barangay Officials of Dontogan Barangay together with the residents share one goal in achieving a peaceful, progressive and Unified Community.

Barangay Dontogan is located in Baguio City. To be more specific, it is the south-westernmost barangay of the city. It lies in the Green Valley subdivision.

Travel time from Baguio to Dontogan would be about 17 min.
