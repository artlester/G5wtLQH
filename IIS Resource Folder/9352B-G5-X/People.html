The successful governance of Dontogan is due to its respective Barangay officials. These individuals who were elected by the people have indeed held up their responsibilites as seen by the changes in the community and the several completion of projects.

The members of the Barangay Council work hand in hand in identifying, planning, preparing and implementing projects which are beneficial to community-development. Priority projects are always being considered so as to respond to the immediate needs of the Community.
