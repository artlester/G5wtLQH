ABOUT

Dontogan Barangay is governed by the Barangay Council headed by the Punong Barangay Henry F. Figueras, 7 Kagawads with their respective Committees, Barangay Secretary and Barangay Treasurer. They are the elected and appointed Barangay officials of Dontogan Barangay. The members of the Barangay Council work hand in hand in identifying, planning, preparing and implementing projects which are beneficial to community-development.
