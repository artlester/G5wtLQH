Nestled at the panoramic and famous Mount Sto. Tomas, Baguio City's highest summit at 5000 feet above sea level-is a community of people who adapted to their environment and to the demands of an ever-changing world keeping alive their own values and traditions from mixed cultures and heritages.

This community of good natured people is called Dontogan, this place is a bit far from the city of Baguio but its nature as a clean and peaceful barangay stands. The state and welfare of the community can be attributed to the good and responsible governance of its local administration.
